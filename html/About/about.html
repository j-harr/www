Biking

Driving

I love to drive my car. I learned to drive on a manual and I have always felt like driving a manual is therapeutic and I appreciate that it takes my mind off of things and helps me to relax. I have gone on many long spontaneous road trips, like to Denver for instance. To save money, I avoid hotels and sleep at rest stops if I need to.

Crocheting

I enjoy crocheting. It is a skill my grandmother taught me when I was younger and I think it is relaxing and rewarding to be able to see a project take shape and eventually reach completion.

Music

Music plays an active part in my everyday life. I am rarely not listening to music and when I am in silence, I consider that music in and of itself.
